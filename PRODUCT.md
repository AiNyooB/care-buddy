## Users

Long-time computer users who need a lightweight tool to naturally build healthy work-rest rhythms during prolonged computer sessions. They want to reduce health issues from prolonged sitting, eye strain, and repetitive stress without disruptive interruptions to their workflow.

## Product Purpose

CareBuddy is a lightweight desktop health reminder app that helps users naturally establish healthy work-rest routines during long computer sessions. It reduces the health burden of prolonged sitting and eye strain through timed reminders, guided micro-exercises, and non-intrusive lock-screen breaks — lowering the barrier to consistent self-care during focused work.

Success means users maintain their healthy habits over months without feeling the app is in their way.

## Anti-references

- Loud or gamified notifications that demand attention
- Cluttered dashboards with metrics and streaks
- Bright or saturated brand colors; the UI stays in the background
- "Smart" features that override user intent without consent
- Any UI pattern that distracts from the user's primary work

## Design Principles

1. **Non-intrusive by default** — The app earns its place; it never demands attention unless health requires it. Notifications are gentle, windows are compact, and lock-screen breaks are escapable.
2. **Natural rhythm, not rigid rules** — The app adapts to idle detection, entertainment mode, and user schedule rather than enforcing fixed intervals. The user is always in control.
3. **Less is more** — Every screen, setting, and notification is there because it directly serves the health goal. Features that add configuration complexity without proportional health benefit are out.
4. **Expert confidence, not novice handholding** — The UI assumes the user can operate their computer. No onboarding tours, no tooltip spam, no "are you sure?" on every toggle.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- Dark and light themes, following system preference by default
- Lock-screen exercise animations with visual and optional voice guidance
- Minimal reliance on color alone for conveying state
- Reduced motion support via `prefers-reduced-motion`
